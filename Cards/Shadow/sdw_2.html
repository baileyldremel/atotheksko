In the Shadow of the Typeface

Struck an idea based on shadows.

Whilst Andy was explaining something in class, I moved my hand with some lego experimentation towards my lamp and I noticed it created a letter. I then moved the object around and it created another letter. I stumbled into this idea of creating multiple letters into a 3D object and by looking at it a certain way, it will create the glyph.
