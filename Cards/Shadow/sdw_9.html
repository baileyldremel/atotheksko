Combining two ideas into one

After the completion of the assignment, we were challenged to combine our rules with another persons rules to create a combined typeface. Myself and Fergus had to combine our two ideas (his was using light to project letters of type) and it resulted in an idea where we use light to project the shape of the 3d form onto a wall. It looked very interesting and kind of gave me horror vibes. I’m pretty sure this idea has been done before in a video game but I can’t remember what one it was.
