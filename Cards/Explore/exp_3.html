ClimateCrisis Typeface

A variable typeface I found that visualises the melting of the icecaps.

Whilst browsing for stuff to read, I ran into this article talking about a typeface that changes shrinks in response to the melting of the icecaps. Made by Daniel Coull and Eino Korkala, this is something that is very interesting for a typeface. It is helping to send a message by the form of the type, not just what is written by the typography itself.
