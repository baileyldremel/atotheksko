Musical Type

What if there was a variable type that responded to music?

A random idea came into my head about variable typeface that responded to music input. For example, the bass of music effects the weight of the font, the vocals effect the height, and the tempo of the song effects makes the typeface lean left and right (like a metronome). I did happen to find a project that uses a variable typeface that responds to music, but from what I can see the typeface is responding to the volume of the sound, not the actual music itself.
