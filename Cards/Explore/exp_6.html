A variable typeface that changes forms

I stumbled upon a thing on Instagram. It was a typeface that is variable not in thickness, but in form and how it looks. The form of the typeface takes the shape of different type styles as it has 4 different forms: Classic, Modern, Loopy and the Default typeface. It would be interesting to see the application of such typeface.
