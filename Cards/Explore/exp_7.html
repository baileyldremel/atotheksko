The NFT Space

Discussing NFT and why it could be so important.

During this weeks session, there was a debate around NFT’s. I already knew about NFT’s but I was thinking if it was possible to sell a font as an NFT and how it could potentially work. Perhaps the font file is only available to that one person and they can use it as they so wish. They could use it in projects so it can gain attention then the owner of the typeface call sell it to the next person at a higher value.
