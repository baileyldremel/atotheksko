Animated Initials

The result of the second week of Hour of Code, where we looked at adding interactions.

After the first week, we had to add something that makes the sketch more interesting (such as shapes following the mouse or changing colour based on the mouse position). I got very carried away and found out how to make shapes expand based on the framerate of the composition. This made the arcs form and the rectangles increase height. I also wrote some code to change the colour of the reference shapes to a random colour every time you clicked.
