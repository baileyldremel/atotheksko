Paper cuts

Creating a paper prototype of my idea

I wanted to depart from my previous project (the 3D type thing) as I wanted to experiment with something else. I got to work on an idea I had where a typeface would be made of binary code (ones and zeroes). I cut out a couple of ones and a zero out of paper and played around with them. Honestly, this looks really good and could be a fun typeface to create.
