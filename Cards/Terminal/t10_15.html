Packing it up

Combining everything for the finale.

Once all the pieces were ready, I got to work on the PDF and the hero image. I took the feedback from the previous project and made sure that my typeface was the focus of the document. I made it the titles, the image captions and chose to use a ‘boring’ typeface (Raleway) to ensure that the focus is on my hard work.


For the hero image, I built upon my sampler and created something that looks like an old computer terminal, with the colour green and the curved scan lines. Why green? From what I had seen, green is often the colour used for computer terminals (other than the classing black and white).
