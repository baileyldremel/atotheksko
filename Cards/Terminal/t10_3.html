Taking a sample

Getting ideas for the sampler

When thinking about my typeface, which is created out of ones and zeros (much like binary type), I thought about an old-school computer terminal where ones and zeros originated. The idea would be to create a sampler that evokes the idea that this is a typeface that is based on the computer. It would have interactions where users can enter commands and the terminal will display content for that command (eg /SAMPLE to show a sample text of the typeface or /GLYPHA for a the letter A).
