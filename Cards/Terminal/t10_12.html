Fixing the leading issues with my sampler

One of the things that was bugging me with my sampler was that the lines would draw over themselves if the previous line broke into two lines. I was experiencing a lot of trouble with this but I managed to sort of fix the issue by planning out my code by writing it in pseudocode, testing it seperately in another sketch then implementing it into the sampler. It works, but it doesn’t take into consideration the idea that a line that doesn’t initally break can break when the user changes the screen size. I could change it to look at the width of the screen instead of a flat number.
