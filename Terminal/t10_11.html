Stumbled upon someone doing a similar idea

As I was browsing Instagram and had stumbled upon something that looked extremely familar. This designer was creating a terminal typeface as well, although it looked very different from my idea. Their typeface looked more like a traditional san-serif typeface with terminal inspiration. This is not what I am going for, as I wanted to create a monospaced typeface based on computer code by using perfect circles and straight lines resembling ones and zeros.
