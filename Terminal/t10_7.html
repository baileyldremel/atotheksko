Numb and Char

Adjustments to the typeface and creating the numbers and other characters.

After some feedback from Andy, I adjusted the slabs of most of the glyphs to make it more readable. I added the numbers and the other characters such as the ampersand and punctuation marks using the same rules as the other characters (boils down to perfect, circular curves and straight lines that look like ones). Some of them were easier than others. I remember Andy spoke about how most designers break rules when they have the make the Ampersand (&) character but I was really proud of my ampersand character.
