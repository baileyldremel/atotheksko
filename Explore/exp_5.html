Witnessing Perspective

Do you see what I see?

Last year I talked about a videogame called ‘The Witness’ where there are these line puzzles on panels that you solve to progress. The main thing that kind of relates to what I am doing for PrototypeType is that there is a puzzle on top of a mountain that matches what the river below. If you enter the puzzle mode on try to solve the river, you will be rewarded. Once that happens, your perspective changes. I began to see circles and lines all over the game and then it began to bleed into my real world. I wanted to bring that kind of experience into this project, making someone have a different perspective on the world
